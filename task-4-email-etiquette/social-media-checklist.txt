Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:
1. Keep your profile professional
2. Use respectful language online
3. Verify information before sharing
4. Maintain privacy settings
5. Build a positive digital image

Don’ts:
1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information
2. Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
3. Don’t spread fake news
4. Don’t engage in unnecessary online arguments
5. Don’t misuse others’ content
