Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unknown UPI payment requests.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be bank or police officials.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media.
5. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
6. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
7. Do not install apps from unknown or untrusted sources.
8. Regularly update your phone and apps to maintain security.
9. Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true.
10. Immediately report any cyber fraud to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call helpline 1930.
